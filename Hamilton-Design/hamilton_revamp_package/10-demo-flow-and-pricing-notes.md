# 10. Demo Flow and Pricing Notes

## Recommended demo flow (5 minutes)

1. Start on Home
   - Show one strong thesis
   - Show What Changed
   - Click Simulate Change

2. Move to Simulate
   - Start at current fee
   - Change proposed fee
   - Show delta in percentile, risk, and median gap
   - Show recommendation

3. Move to Report
   - Show executive summary
   - Export PDF

4. Move to Monitor
   - Show Hamilton watching peers and regulatory signals
   - Reinforce renewal value

## What to say
- "Hamilton tells you what changed."
- "Hamilton helps you understand what it means."
- "Hamilton models the tradeoffs before you move pricing."
- "Hamilton gives you a board-ready output."

## Pricing framing
Do not sell:
- AI queries
- dashboards
- data access

Sell:
- pricing decision support
- peer positioning intelligence
- regulatory-risk visibility
- board-ready outputs

## Suggested packaging
- Core / Analyst
- Pro / Decision
- Enterprise / Intelligence
