# 03. Screen Specs

## Screen 1 — Home / Executive Briefing

### Job
Orientation and prioritization.

### Required modules
- Hamilton's View
- What Changed
- Why It Matters
- Priority Alerts
- Recommended Action
- Positioning Evidence
- Monitor Feed

### Primary CTA
Simulate Change

### Rules
- Must feel like a briefing, not a chat answer
- Must have one dominant thesis
- Must tell the user what to do next in one click

---

## Screen 2 — Analyze

### Job
Understanding and exploration.

### Required modules
- Analysis title
- Hamilton's View
- What This Means
- Why It Matters
- Evidence
- Explore Further

### Required CTA hierarchy
1. Simulate a Change
2. Show Peer Distribution
3. View Risk Drivers

### Must not contain
- final recommendation
- board summary CTA
- definitive recommended range

### Prompt examples
- How does this compare to similar-sized banks?
- When did we become an outlier?
- Which peers moved first?
- What is driving complaint alignment?

---

## Screen 3 — Simulate

### Job
Decision-making.

### Required modules
- Scenario Setup
- Current vs Proposed
- Hamilton's Interpretation
- Strategic Tradeoffs
- Recommended Position
- Generate Board Scenario Summary

### Required interactivity
- Proposed fee input or slider
- Live updates on percentile, median gap, risk profile, and tradeoff state
- Strong visual contrast between Current and Proposed

### Owns
- recommendation
- tradeoffs
- recommended range

---

## Screen 4 — Report

### Job
Communication and executive presentation.

### Required modules
- Executive Summary
- Current vs Proposed Snapshot
- Strategic Rationale
- Tradeoff Summary
- Recommended Position
- Implementation Notes
- Export controls

### Rules
- Read-only
- No sliders
- No exploratory prompts
- No unnecessary controls

---

## Screen 5 — Monitor

### Job
Retention and continuous intelligence.

### Required modules
- Status strip
- Priority Alert
- Signal Feed
- Active Watchlists

### Keep light
- Watchlists in side rail or right rail
- Signals as mini insights, not raw log lines

### Remove or simplify
- heavy trend widget
- duplicate dashboard content
- too many competing panels
