| T-28-04 | Elevation of Privilege | pro/layout.tsx | mitigate | canAccessPremium() checks subscription_status === 'active' OR admin/analyst role. Non-premium authenticated users redirect to /subscribe. |
| T-28-05 | Information Disclosure | ProNav props | accept | Only user.display_name, institution_name initial, and PersonalizationContext (derived from their own profile) are passed to client component. No sensitive data exposed. |
