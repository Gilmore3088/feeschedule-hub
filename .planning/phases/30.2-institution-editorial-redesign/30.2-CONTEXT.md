<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### ALL DECISIONS ARE LOCKED — from the mockup and DESIGN.md

- **D-01:** "No-Line Rule" — 1px solid borders prohibited for sectioning. Use background color shifts and negative space only.
- **D-02:** Parchment palette: surface #fbf9f4 (not #FAF7F2), surface-container-low #f5f3ee, surface-container-high #eae8e3, primary #8a4c27 (burnt sienna, not terracotta #C44B2E)
- **D-03:** Two-column rating grid: left col (5/12) = rating card with big overdraft number + insight bullets. Right col (7/12) = editorial quote + 3 stat cards + inline pro CTA.
- **D-04:** Fee schedule uses collapsible `<details>` sections per category with expand/collapse
- **D-05:** Each fee row shows a market distribution mini-bar (position dot on a range bar showing P25-P75)
- **D-06:** Overdraft & NSF section open by default, others collapsed
- **D-07:** "Institution Intelligence" label above institution name (not "Institution Profile")
- **D-08:** Institution name in massive Newsreader serif (text-5xl md:text-6xl)
- **D-09:** Pro CTA is an inline card within the right column — NOT a separate section
- **D-10:** "For Financial Professionals" bottom section with 3 bullet capabilities + gradient CTA button
- **D-11:** Methodology footer at very bottom, muted opacity-60
- **D-12:** Material Symbols Outlined icons (location_on, account_balance, analytics, etc.)
